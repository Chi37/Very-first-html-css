About Me

Throughout my college career, I have been involved with building bridges between cultures.
I have been a part of Project Pengyou, an organization that aims to connect American and Chinese culture.
I have also been a part of other organizations such as the Vietnamese-Student Association
and the Korean-American Associatioin on campus. I also have traveled to Taiwan to lead a roundtable discussion and traveled abroad in Italy.


I studied in International Studies and got a certificate in Education and Educational Services thinking I was going to work for the government with exchange programs. However, that did not work out as I got rejected from so many places in the East Coast.


I was abroad in Beijing, China last August to attend a month long program at Tsinghua University. My team and I worked on a project to reduce waste water and reuse rain water. We talked about Sponge Cities, which just means adding greenery to pavements in the cities to absorb and filter water.
